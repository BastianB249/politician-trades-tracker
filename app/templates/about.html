About Capitol Trades Tracker

Capitol Trades Tracker aggregates public financial disclosure reports from US lawmakers. These disclosures can be delayed and are not real-time trading signals.

How performance is computed

For each disclosed buy transaction, we compute a simple return over 1-year and 5-year windows using local price snapshots. We also compute the same return for the S&P 500 ETF (SPY) over the exact window, and the reported "excess return" is the difference between those two averages.
